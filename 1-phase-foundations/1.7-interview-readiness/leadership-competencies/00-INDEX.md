## The Director Competency Framework

Five competencies that separate Directors from Senior Managers:

**1. Systems Thinking Over Task Thinking**
ICs fix the problem. Directors fix the system that allowed the problem.

**2. Business Translation**
ICs speak in uptime and latency. Directors speak in revenue protected and risk reduced.

**3. Influence at Scale**
ICs influence their team. Directors influence teams they don't manage, executives they report to, and vendors they depend on.

**4. Decision Architecture**
ICs make decisions. Directors build the system that allows their team to make decisions without escalating everything.

**5. Talent Multiplication**
ICs do great work. Directors make 15 people do great work — and build the environment where that continues after they leave.
