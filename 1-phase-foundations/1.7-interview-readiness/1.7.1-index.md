# Management Principles — Director-Level Learning Path

## Purpose

This library is built for the Senior Manager → Director/VP transition. Every module is operational-first: tools you pick up and use in a staff meeting, a budget conversation, or a quarterly business review — not theory you admire and forget.

Each file pairs the framework with a T-Mobile SRE / telecom operations anchor so the model is already translated into your context.

## The Director Mental Model (30 seconds)

As a Senior Manager, your job was: **execute well and develop your team.**

As a Director, your job shifts to: **design the system that makes execution possible.**

You stop doing the work. You stop managing people who do the work. You **manage the managers** who manage people who do the work. Every tool in this library is a lever for that — not for you to use directly, but for you to install in the managers below you so the system runs without you in the room.
