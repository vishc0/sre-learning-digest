## Interview Readiness Check

You are principal-ready when you can:
- Explain reliability tradeoffs clearly in business terms.
- Lead high-severity incidents and drive durable fixes.
- Design SLO frameworks and enforce error-budget policy.
- Influence engineering roadmaps through quantified reliability risk.
- Scale teams and reliability culture across multiple domains.
