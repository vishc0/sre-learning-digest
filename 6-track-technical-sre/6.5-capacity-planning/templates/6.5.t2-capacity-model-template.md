## 7. Assumptions and Risks

### Key Assumptions

| Assumption | Basis | Confidence |
|------------|-------|-----------|
| 10% monthly traffic growth (organic) | 90-day trend, consistent for 6 months | Medium — assumes no major product changes |
| RF=3 maintained on Cassandra | Current configuration, no plans to change | High |
| No major new services onboarding to shared cluster | Confirmed with product roadmap | Medium — roadmap is 6-month visibility only |
| Holiday 2026 campaign: 3.2× multiplier | Based on Holiday 2025 actuals | High for volume; timing may vary |
| SMPP TPS increase approved by Carrier A | In negotiation — not confirmed | Low — confirm by 2026-07-15 |

### Risks That Would Invalidate This Model

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Early Warning Signal |
|------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| New product launches faster than forecast | Medium | High — sudden traffic step-up | Product roadmap reviews; capacity calendar updates |
| Growth rate accelerates beyond 10%/month | Medium | High — all action dates move earlier | Monthly trend check; alert if 30-day growth >15% |
| Cassandra partition hot spot develops | Low | High — isolated node hits disk fast | Per-node disk divergence alert (>10% spread between highest and lowest node) |
| Carrier SMPP TPS increase denied | Medium | Medium — campaigns must throttle | Carrier negotiation status (check weekly) |
| Team capacity (SREs) insufficient to execute expansion | Medium | Medium — delays expand timeline | Headcount planning; expansion tasks vs team bandwidth |
