## 3. Per-Namespace Capacity Table

*Collect via Prometheus queries or `kubectl top pods -n <namespace>` aggregated by namespace.*  
*Request Efficiency = (Actual P95 / Requested) × 100. Target: 60-80%.*  
*Limit:Request Ratio = CPU Limit / CPU Request. Target: 1.0-2.0.*

| Namespace | CPU Req (vCPU) | CPU Limit (vCPU) | CPU Actual P95 | Mem Req (GB) | Mem Limit (GB) | Mem Actual P95 | Request Efficiency % | Limit:Request Ratio | Risk Flag |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|---------------|-------------|---------------|---------------|---------------------|-------------------|-----------|
| **dnd-notification** | 24 | 48 | 19.2 | 96 | 128 | 74 | 80% | 2.0 | 🟢 Well-sized |
| **api-gateway** | 8 | 12 | 5.2 | 32 | 48 | 19 | 65% | 1.5 | 🟢 Well-sized |
| **reporting-batch** | 32 | 64 | 6.4 | 64 | 128 | 12 | 20% | 2.0 | 🔴 Over-requested — reclaim |
| **legacy-integration** | 16 | 16 | 12.8 | 48 | 48 | 46 | 80% | 1.0 | 🟡 No burst headroom — OOM risk |
| **dev-scratchpad** | 8 | 32 | 0.4 | 16 | 64 | 1.2 | 5% | 4.0 | 🔴 Dead workload — reclaim |

**Example legend**:
- `dnd-notification`: well-sized — actual usage is 80% of requests, limit allows 2× burst. Target range.
- `api-gateway`: well-sized — 65% efficiency, moderate burst headroom.
- `reporting-batch`: over-requested — 20% efficiency. 25.6 vCPU being claimed but not used. This is wasted scheduling capacity.
- `legacy-integration`: no burst headroom — CPU limit equals CPU request. Under normal load this looks fine, but any spike causes immediate CPU throttling. Memory limit equals request — OOMKill risk during GC pause.
- `dev-scratchpad`: dead workload — 5% efficiency over 90 days. This namespace should be reviewed for retirement.
