## Status Color Definitions

| Color | Definition | VP Action Required |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| Green | On schedule, within budget (< 5% variance), no unresolved blockers | Review only |
| Yellow | Schedule slipped 1–2 weeks, budget variance 5–15%, or blocker unresolved > 5 days | Acknowledge within 24 hours |
| Red | Schedule slipped > 2 weeks, budget variance > 15%, blocker unresolved > 10 days, or traffic rollout stalled | Decision within 48 hours |

A transformation that stays Red for two consecutive weeks without a recovery plan escalates to the Engineering Director for portfolio intervention.

## Traffic Rollout Gates

Traffic advancement requires VP sign-off at the following thresholds:

| Gate | Traffic % | Required Evidence Before Advancing |
|------|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| Canary entry | 0% → ≤ 10% | Build passing, smoke tests green, dashboards live |
| Progressive entry | 10% → 25% | 7-day SLO at canary ≥ legacy baseline, no open P1/P2 |
| Progressive mid | 25% → 75% | 14-day SLO continuous compliance, data reconciliation ≥ 99.99% |
| Cutover | 75% → 100% | 30-day SLO compliance, runbooks complete, rollback tested |
| Decommission-pending | 100% | 90-day SLO compliance, all consumers confirmed migrated |

Any gate failure halts advancement. Rollback to the previous stable percentage is automatic when the new system SLO drops below legacy baseline for > 30 consecutive minutes.
