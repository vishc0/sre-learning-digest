# SRE: The Missing Layer — A Framework for Reliability Engineering

## What This Is

Standard SRE frameworks measure per-service reliability. Customer experience is determined by compound reliability across the call chains those customers actually traverse. This is the gap no current toolset addresses.

This framework introduces 25 coined terms and their formulae that make the gap between declared SLOs and actual customer experience visible, quantifiable, and governable — across steady-state operations, change events, migrations, and service lifecycle transitions.

The framework is built on a 12-service e-commerce shopping cart as the reference system. Every formula is computed against that system with real numbers. Every decision gate is demonstrated with real data.

## The 25 Coined Terms — Complete Index

### Structural Cluster (Chapter 2)

| # | Term | Abbrev | Formula | What it answers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Call Depth | CD | `count(sequential_sync_hops)` | How many failure points are in this call chain? |
| 2 | SLO Coherence Score | SCS | `SLO^CD / customer_SLA` | Is the math behind our SLA actually sound? |
| 3 | Latency Budget Per Hop | LBH | `(e2e_p99 - overhead) / CD` | What p99 must each service hit? |

### Blast Radius Cluster (Chapter 3)

| # | Term | Abbrev | Formula | What it answers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 4 | Dependency Gravity | DG | `Σ[tw × proximity_discount]` | How dangerous is this service to fail? |
| 5 | Blast Radius Index | BRI | `(affected% × criticality × tw)` | What SEV is this incident? |
| 6 | Cascade Coefficient | CC | `Σ[P(cascade) × tw]` | Will this failure spread? |
| 7 | Failure Locality Index | FLI | `contained / total` | Did it spread? Are our defenses working? |
| 8 | Dependency Surface Area | DSA | `Σ[1×tw_direct] + Σ[0.5×tw_transitive]` | How exposed is this service to others' failures? |

### Health Signal Cluster (Chapter 4)

| # | Term | Abbrev | Formula | What it answers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 9 | Error Budget Velocity | EBV | `normalized_errors / baseline` | How fast are we burning budget? |
| 10 | Alert Precision Rate | APR | `actionable / total_alerts` | Is our alerting signal or noise? |
| 11 | Mean Time Between Incidents | MTBI | `period / incident_count` | What is the actual reliability vs. declared? |
| 12 | Observability Coverage Ratio | OCR | `Σ(instrumented×tw) / Σ(tw)` | How much of the system can we see? |

### Efficiency Cluster (Chapter 5)

| # | Term | Abbrev | Formula | What it answers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 13 | Operational Leverage | OL | `Δbudget_pp / SRE_hours` | Which investment moves the needle most? |
| 14 | Recovery Cost Ratio | RCR | `(response_hrs × freq) / fix_hrs` | Should we fix this or defer it? |
| 15 | Toil Amplification Factor | TAF | `toil_growth / traffic_growth` | Is our toil scaling sustainably? |

### Change Governance Cluster (Chapter 6)

| # | Term | Abbrev | Formula | What it answers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16 | Service Change Volatility | SCV | `freq × CSAI × failure_rate` | How risky is this service's deployment pattern? |
| 17 | Migration Risk Index | MRI | `DG × CD × (1-FLI) × (2-OCR)` | Which CAB tier does this change require? |
| 18 | Change Surface Delta | CSD | `Σ[tw × depth_coeff]` | How wide is this change's footprint? |
| 19 | Rollback Velocity | RV | measured rollback time | How long must the maintenance window be? |
| 20 | Service Compatibility Window | SCW | `max(deploy, consumer_upgrade, migration)` | How long must two versions coexist? |
| 21 | Error Budget Reservation | EBR | `min(MRI × 0.05, 0.80)` | Is there enough budget to absorb this change failing? |
| 22 | Deployment Safety Score | DSS | `(1-SCV_n)×(1-MRI/10)×APR×budget%` | What monitoring level does this deployment need? |

### Lifecycle Cluster (Chapter 7)

| # | Term | Abbrev | Formula | What it answers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 23 | Greenfield Exposure Index | GEI | `(new_deps×avg_tw)/(coverage×canary_hrs)` | Is this new service ready to join the system? |
| 24 | Rollout Signal Threshold | RST | `rollout% where z-score ≥ 2` | When can I trust the feature flag signal? |
| 25 | Protocol Coexistence Cost | PCC | `dual_ops_hrs/day × rate × days` | When does dual-version support become economically forced to end? |
| 26 | Scale Lead Time Deficit | SLTD | `(ramp_rps/min × lag_min) / pod_capacity` | How many pods must be pre-provisioned? |
| 27 | Simultaneous SLO Exposure | SSE | `(in_cutover/total) × avg(1-SLO_margin)` | Is the migration moving too fast? |
| 28 | Patch Parallelism Ceiling | PPC | `floor((window-validation)/rollback_time)` | How many services can be patched simultaneously? |
| 29 | Recurrence Displacement Rate | RDR | `new_mode_rate / old_mode_rate` | Did the fix eliminate or just move the problem? |
| 30 | Shadow Coupling Index | SCI | `(observed - declared) / total_observed` | Are there hidden callers that will break on decommission? |
| 31 | External Deprecation Forcing Date | EDFD | `today + (EBR_reserved / daily_EBV_burn)` | When must the third-party migration complete? |

### Composite Scores (Chapter 8)

| Score | Formula | What it answers |
| --- | --- | --- |
| RIS (Reliability Investment Score) | `(RCR × OL) / MRI` | Which backlog item has the best risk-adjusted return? |
| SRMI (System Reliability Maturity Index) | `(FLI × OCR × APR/0.85) / (TAF × CC_max/10)` | How mature is our reliability practice overall? |
| DSS (Deployment Safety Score) | `(1-SCV_n) × (1-MRI/10) × APR × budget%` | Is it safe to deploy right now? |
| JRCS (Journey Reliability Coherence Score) | `SCS × FLI × OCR_path × (1-CC_max/10)` | Is this customer journey actually reliable end-to-end? |

| Metric | Current Value | Target | Chapter |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Checkout actual availability | 99.62% | > 99.92% | 1, 2 |
| Checkout declared SLA | 99.95% | — | 1 |
| OCR_weighted | 45.7% | > 95% | 4 |
| APR | 31% | > 80% | 4 |
| FLI | 72% | > 90% | 3 |
| CC_max (Auth) | 10.8 | < 2.0 | 3 |
| TAF | 0.62 | < 0.40 | 5 |
| SRMI | 0.179 | > 5.0 | 8 |
| JRCS (Checkout) | Negative | > 0.80 | 8 |

**Why JRCS is negative**: CC_max = 10.8 means the (1 - CC_max/10) term = -0.08. When cascade risk exceeds the safety threshold, the journey coherence score goes negative — indicating systemic cascade risk regardless of whether any individual service is currently failing.

## Central Thesis

**SRE frameworks fail organizations when they treat services in isolation.**

Every current SRE toolset measures per-service reliability. No toolset currently measures how per-service reliability compounds across the call chains that real users traverse, or how changes to individual components ripple through those chains during migrations, feature launches, and platform upgrades.

This framework introduces the missing layer: 25 cross-service, cross-journey, cross-scenario measurements that make the gap between declared SLOs and actual customer experience visible, quantifiable, and governable.
