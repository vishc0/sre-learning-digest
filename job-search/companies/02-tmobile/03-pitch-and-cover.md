## The Internal Conversation (Not a Cover Letter)
This isn't an application — it's a conversation with your manager.

### How to Start the Conversation
"I want to have a direct conversation about my growth path at T-Mobile. I've been at Principal level for several years, I'm managing 4 platforms and 15 engineers, and I've been operating at what I consider Director scope for some time. I want to understand: is there a path to Director or Distinguished Engineer within the notification org, and what's the timeline? I want to make that move here, but I want to be thoughtful about it."

### What to Prepare
1. A 1-page "scope document" showing current impact vs. Director-level expectations
2. Market comp data (use numbers from this folder as leverage)
3. 2-3 specific Director-level initiatives you're ready to lead
4. Clarity on what you're asking for: title, scope, comp, or all three

### If Internal Path Is Unclear or Too Slow
"I want to be transparent: I'm at a stage in my career where if there isn't a clear internal path to Director or Principal Architect in the next 6-12 months, I'll need to explore external options. I'm not threatening — I'm being honest so we can plan together."
