## Verbal Handoff Script

> Read this aloud on the bridge before the outgoing responder disconnects. Target: 90 seconds.

---

**"Attention bridge — this is [OUTGOING NAME], handing off to [INCOMING NAME] at [TIME] UTC."**

**"Where we are:"**
> State current phase, severity, and how long the incident has been running. One sentence on what customers are experiencing right now.

**"What we know:"**
> Name the leading hypothesis. Name the one or two strongest pieces of evidence supporting it. Name what has been ruled out and why in one sentence.

**"What we don't know:"**
> State the open question that would most change the response direction if answered. State what is being done right now to answer it.

**"What to watch for:"**
> Call out the most important landmine. Name the in-flight action most likely to change the situation in the next 15 minutes. State the next scheduled status update time.

**"[INCOMING NAME] has confirmed the handoff. I am dropping from the bridge now. Reach me at [CONTACT] if needed."**

---

> Incoming responder: after the outgoing responder drops, announce yourself: "This is [NAME], I have IC / TL / SME. Continuing."
