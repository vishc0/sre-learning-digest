### Impact Score Selection

Select the **highest** tier that any single factor reaches — do not average.

- [ ] **P1 — Critical** — More than 10% of customers affected, OR revenue impact exceeds $1M/hour, OR regulatory/legal violation is possible, OR emergency services infrastructure is in blast radius
- [ ] **P2 — High** — 1–10% of customers affected, OR revenue $100K–$1M/hour, OR SLA breach with contractual consequence
- [ ] **P3 — Medium** — Less than 1% of customers affected, OR revenue below $100K/hour, OR internal SLA only (no contractual consequence)
- [ ] **P4 — Low** — No customer impact; change affects dev, test, internal tooling, or non-production environments only

## Section 2: Likelihood of Failure Scoring

Rate each factor. Use the factor scores to select an overall Likelihood Score.

| Factor | High Risk | Medium Risk | Low Risk | Your Score |
|--------|-----------|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Code path coverage** | New untested code path | Modified existing path, tested once | Unchanged component; config or dependency version only | |
| **Staging environment parity** | No staging environment or no parity with production data volumes/traffic | Some parity; known gaps exist | Production-equivalent staging; last validated within 72 hours | |
| **Rollback complexity** | Multi-step rollback; requires coordination across teams; data migration required | Moderate rollback; 2–3 steps, one team | Simple revert under 5 minutes; single command, single operator | |
| **Change operator experience** | First time operating this system or first time executing this change type | Familiar with system; executed similar change before | Expert; has executed this exact change type multiple times in production | |
| **External dependency count** | 3 or more external dependencies in the change execution path | 1–2 external dependencies | No external dependencies; fully self-contained | |
| **Recent change history on this service** | Service had a change failure or incident in the past 30 days | 1–2 changes in past 30 days with no failures | No recent changes; or 6+ months of stable change history | |
| **Data mutation** | Change creates, modifies, or deletes persistent data as part of execution | Change reads data or updates non-critical data | Change is stateless; no data is touched | |

### Likelihood Score Selection

- [ ] **High** — 3 or more High Risk factor scores, OR any factor is High Risk AND the impact is P1
- [ ] **Medium** — Mostly Medium Risk factors; no more than 1 High Risk factor; no P1 impact
- [ ] **Low** — Mostly Low Risk factors; 0–1 Medium Risk factor; no High Risk factors

### How Controls Shift the Risk Tier

Each active mitigating control reduces the effective Likelihood Score:
- **Feature flag + automated rollback** together: reduce Likelihood by one tier (High → Medium, Medium → Low)
- **Canary or blue-green** alone: reduce Likelihood by one tier
- **Rollback tested in staging**: confirm Low Likelihood scoring for rollback complexity factor
- **No staging parity**: cannot reduce Likelihood Score regardless of other controls
