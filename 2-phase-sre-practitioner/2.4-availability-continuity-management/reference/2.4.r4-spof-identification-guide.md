# SPOF Identification Guide

A systematic checklist for identifying Single Points of Failure across all dimensions of a service or system. Use this during architecture reviews, quarterly SPOF audits, and DR planning.

**For each item: answer Yes (redundancy exists) / No (SPOF exists) / Unknown (needs investigation). Any "No" or "Unknown" is a finding.**

---

## How to Use This Guide

1. Work through each section with the service owner and infrastructure lead
2. Mark "No" for every component that has no redundancy
3. Mark "Unknown" if you cannot confirm redundancy exists — treat Unknown as No until confirmed
4. Score each SPOF using the risk scoring at the bottom
5. Prioritize SPOFs for remediation using the risk score

## SPOF Risk Scoring

For each "No" or "Unknown" identified:

### Step 1: Score Probability of Failure

| Score | Probability | Description |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| 3 | High | Component fails > 3 times/year or vendor SLA < 99.9% |
| 2 | Medium | Component fails 1–3 times/year or vendor SLA 99.9%–99.99% |
| 1 | Low | Component fails < once/year or vendor SLA > 99.99% |

### Step 2: Score Impact of Failure

| Score | Impact | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 3 | Critical | Complete outage of Tier 1 or Tier 2 service |
| 2 | Major | Partial outage; significant degradation |
| 1 | Minor | Degraded service; no complete outage |

### Step 3: Score Exposure Time

| Score | Exposure | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| 3 | Long | Detection > 10 min or recovery > 30 min |
| 2 | Medium | Detection 2–10 min or recovery 5–30 min |
| 1 | Short | Automated detection < 2 min and automated recovery < 5 min |

### Risk Score = Probability × Impact × Exposure

| Score Range | Priority | Action |
|------------|----------|--------|
| 18–27 | P1 — Critical | Fund remediation immediately; interim compensating control required |
| 8–17 | P2 — High | Remediate within 90 days; include in next budget cycle |
| 3–7 | P3 — Medium | Remediate within 6 months; track in SPOF register |
| 1–2 | P4 — Low | Document and accept; review annually |

Maintain a SPOF register updated quarterly:

| SPOF-002 | DBA (sole backup restore knowledge) | People | 1 | 3 | 3 | 9 | P2 | SRE Lead | Cross-train second DBA; document restore procedure | [date] | Open |
