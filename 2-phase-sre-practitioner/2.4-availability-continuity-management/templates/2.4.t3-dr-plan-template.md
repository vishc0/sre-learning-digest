## Section 8: Communication Templates

### Status Page — Initial Update (Publish within 15 minutes of declaration)
```
[Time] — We are aware of an issue affecting [service name]. Our teams are investigating 
and have activated our disaster recovery procedures. We will provide an update in 30 minutes.
```

### Status Page — Recovery in Progress
```
[Time] — Our disaster recovery procedures are actively in progress. 
Estimated restoration: [time or "TBD — update in 30 minutes"].
```

### Status Page — Recovered
```
[Time] — Services have been restored. We will publish a full incident report within 24 hours.
```

### Enterprise Customer Email Template
```
Subject: [Company] Service Disruption Notice — [Service Name]

Dear [Account Name],

At [time], we experienced a service disruption affecting [service name]. 
[2–3 sentences describing impact].

We activated our disaster recovery procedures at [time] and restored service at [time] 
(total impact: [duration]).

[If SLA breach: We recognize this event may trigger the SLA provisions in your agreement. 
Our customer success team will reach out within 2 business days to discuss.]

A full incident report will be provided within 24 hours.

[Signed — VP of Operations or Customer Success VP]
```
