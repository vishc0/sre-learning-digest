## Instructions for Interviewers

1. Complete Section 1 (function inventory) with the business stakeholder. Do not suggest functions — let them identify them.
2. Complete Section 2 (impact quantification) using the financial model prepared before the interview.
3. In Section 3, do not accept "it depends" as an answer — probe until you have a specific MTPD.
4. Complete Section 4 (system mapping) with the technology liaison after the interview.
5. Have the business stakeholder sign Section 5 to confirm accuracy before closing.

## Section 3: MTPD and Workaround Assessment

Complete for each function. This section determines RTO and RPO derivation.

### Function: [Function Name]

**Stated MTPD:** [X hours]

**Is there a manual workaround available if the supporting system is unavailable?**
- [ ] Yes — describe: [What the team would do manually]
- [ ] No — immediate system dependency; no workaround

**If yes: How long can the manual workaround be sustained?** [X hours]

**What limits the manual workaround?**
- [ ] Volume — too many transactions to process manually beyond [X hours]
- [ ] Data access — manual process requires data that is only available in the system
- [ ] Regulatory — manual process does not meet regulatory requirements
- [ ] Staff capacity — insufficient staff to sustain manual process
- [ ] Other: [describe]

**What is the maximum acceptable data loss?**
"If the system were restored right now but the last [X hours/minutes] of data were lost, could your team recover that data from other sources (emails, printed forms, source systems)?"
- Recoverable from other sources for: [X hours] → implies RPO = [X hours]
- Not recoverable beyond: [Y minutes] → implies RPO = [Y minutes]
