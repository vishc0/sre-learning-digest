## 7. Findings

### Finding Severity Definitions

- **Critical**: Would have resulted in MTPD breach, regulatory violation, or data loss in a real DR event
- **High**: Would have materially delayed recovery, increasing actual RTO by > 25%
- **Medium**: Caused inefficiency or confusion; manageable within recovery window
- **Low**: Minor gap; does not affect RTO/RPO outcomes
