### Remote Work Capability Assessment

| Function | Remote-Capable? | Constraints |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| Software engineering | Yes | VPN access required |
| Operations / SRE | Yes | VPN + MFA + approved device required |
| Executive leadership | Yes | Secure communication channel required |
| Data center physical operations | No | Physical presence required; alternate staff identified |
| Regulated data handling | Partial | Home environment must meet security standards |

### Facility Activation Procedure

1. Facilities manager assesses primary facility status
2. Facilities manager notifies VP of Operations of unavailability
3. VP of Operations activates alternate arrangement per scenario above
4. HR notifies all employees of alternate arrangement with specific instructions
5. IT ensures VPN, cloud tools, and remote access are operational for all affected staff
