# SLM Metrics and KPIs

## The VP's Metric Set

The VP of Operations needs a coherent set of metrics that answer one question: "Is the SLM program protecting the organization's commercial commitments?" The metrics below are organized by category: operational health (current state), compliance (SLA performance), program health (SLM program itself), and commercial (customer-level outcomes).

---

## Category 1: Operational Health Metrics

These metrics measure the current state of reliability across the service portfolio.

### Availability by Tier (Current Month to Date)

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Gold tier availability (MTD) | Availability % for all Gold services, current calendar month | ≥ SLA commitment + 0.05% | Daily |
| Silver tier availability (MTD) | Availability % for all Silver services, current calendar month | ≥ SLA commitment + 0.05% | Weekly |
| Bronze tier availability (MTD) | Availability % for all Bronze services, current calendar month | ≥ SLO target | Monthly |
| Services within 20% of error budget exhaustion | Count of services where remaining error budget < 20% of monthly allocation | 0 | Daily |
| Services with error budget exhausted | Count of services with 0% error budget remaining | 0 | Daily |

### Error Budget Burn Rate

| Metric | Definition | Target | Alert Threshold |
|--------|-----------|--------|----------------|
| 7-day burn rate (per service) | Error budget consumed in last 7 days ÷ allowable 7-day consumption | < 1.0× (consuming at sustainable rate) | > 2.0× for 72+ hours |
| Projected month-end availability | Current burn rate projected to month-end | Above SLA commitment | < SLA + 0.01% |
| Burn rate trend | Week-over-week change in burn rate | Stable or decreasing | Any week with > 50% increase in burn rate |

### Incident Impact on SLM

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Incidents contributing to SLO burn (count) | Incidents that consumed error budget in the period | Decreasing trend | Monthly |
| Average budget consumption per incident | Error budget minutes consumed per incident | Decreasing trend | Monthly |
| % of budget consumed by single-incident events | Incidents consuming > 25% of monthly budget in a single event | < 10% | Monthly |

## Category 2: SLA Compliance Metrics

These metrics measure performance against commercial commitments.

### Breach Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Escalation Threshold |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| SLA breaches (count, MTD) | Number of active SLAs in breach status for the current measurement period | 0 | Any breach for a top-10 account |
| SLA breaches (count, trailing 90 days) | Number of breaches across all SLAs in the last 90 days | 0 | ≥ 1 per service per 90-day period |
| Repeat breaches (same service, 12 months) | Count of services with ≥ 2 breaches in rolling 12 months | 0 | Any |
| Time to breach notification | Elapsed time from breach declaration to customer notification | ≤ SLA-specified window (typically 4 hours) | Any notification > SLA window |
| Breach notification on-time rate | % of breach notifications delivered within the SLA notification window | 100% | < 100% |

### Credit Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Total credits issued (month) | Sum of all SLA credits issued in the calendar month | $0 (no breaches) | Monthly |
| Total credits issued (YTD) | Cumulative credits issued year-to-date | Budget against commercial plan | Quarterly |
| Credit issuance cycle time | Days from breach confirmation to credit processing in Finance | ≤ 5 business days | Per breach |
| Credits as % of ARR | Total credits issued ÷ annual recurring revenue | < 0.5% of ARR | Quarterly |
| Disputed credits (count) | Credits disputed by customers | 0 | Monthly |

### SLA Coverage Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Active SLAs (count) | Total SLAs currently in force | Track for growth | Monthly |
| SLAs approaching renewal (< 90 days) | Count of SLAs expiring within 90 days | 0 (all in renewal process) | Monthly |
| SLAs without current SLO backing | Count of SLAs covering services without an approved SLO | 0 | Monthly |
| SLA vs. SLO buffer compliance | % of SLA-backed services where SLO provides ≥ 0.05% buffer above SLA | 100% | Quarterly |

## Category 3: Program Health Metrics

These metrics measure the health of the SLM program itself.

### Service Catalog Health

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Catalog completeness | % of production services with an active catalog entry | 100% | Quarterly |
| Catalog currency — Service Owner | % of entries where Service Owner is a current employee in the named role | 100% | Quarterly |
| Catalog currency — Runbook | % of entries where runbook link is accessible and dated < 12 months | ≥ 95% | Quarterly |
| Catalog currency — Dependencies | % of entries where dependency list matches last architecture review | ≥ 90% | Quarterly |
| New services without pre-launch catalog entry | Count of services that entered production without a catalog entry | 0 | Monthly |

### SLO Review Health

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| SLO reviews completed on schedule | % of quarterly SLO reviews completed by scheduled date | 100% | Quarterly |
| Past-due SLO reviews | Count of services where last SLO review is > 120 days ago | 0 | Quarterly |
| SLO changes approved (count) | Number of SLO modifications made in the quarter | Track for trend | Quarterly |
| SLO changes without VP approval (Gold/Silver) | Count of Gold/Silver SLO changes not approved by VP | 0 | Quarterly |

### Service Reporting Health

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Monthly service reports delivered on time | % of reports delivered by 10th business day of following month | 100% | Monthly |
| Customer disputes of service report data | Count of customers who disputed the availability number in a service report | 0 | Monthly |
| QBRs completed on schedule | % of scheduled QBRs completed on time | ≥ 95% | Quarterly |

### Error Budget Policy Compliance

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Policy overrides (count) | Number of error budget policy overrides in the quarter | Track; document each | Quarterly |
| Policy overrides resulting in incidents | Count of overrides that were followed within 72 hours by a reliability incident | 0 (target; track for calibration) | Per override |
| Deployment freeze violations | Deployments to production during active deployment freeze | 0 | Weekly during freeze periods |

## Category 4: Commercial and Customer Metrics

These metrics measure the business outcomes of the SLM program.

### Customer Satisfaction

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| SLM-related customer satisfaction score | Customer satisfaction score from post-QBR or post-breach surveys | ≥ 8/10 | Per QBR / post-breach |
| Customers requesting SLA downgrade | Count of customers requesting a lower-tier SLA (signal of relationship deterioration) | 0 | Monthly |
| Customers requesting SLA upgrade | Count of customers requesting a higher-tier SLA (signal of confidence) | Track for commercial opportunity | Monthly |
| SLA renewal rate | % of expiring SLAs renewed without renegotiation | ≥ 90% | Annual |

### Commercial Risk

| Metric | Definition | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Customers in repeat breach risk | Count of customers who have experienced ≥ 2 breaches in 12 months | 0 | Monthly |
| Customers invoking escalation rights | Count of customers who have formally invoked contract escalation rights | 0 | Monthly |
| SLM-attributed churn | Customer contracts lost where SLA breaches were a contributing factor | 0 | Annual |
| Unresolved breach remediation commitments | Count of remediation commitments (from breach letters) not completed by due date | 0 | Monthly |

## Metric Reporting Format for VP Dashboard

The VP's weekly SLM dashboard should present:

| Section | Metrics Included | Format |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Portfolio Availability | MTD availability by tier, services at risk | RAG status table |
| Breach Risk | Services within 20% of error budget, projected breach risk | Alert list |
| Open Breaches | Active breach records, notification status, credit status | Table |
| Open Remediation | Past-due commitments, upcoming deadlines | Table |
| Upcoming SLA Renewals | Renewals within 90 days | List |
| Program Health | Catalog, SLO review, reporting health | Score / RAG |

## Metric Ownership

| Metric Category | Owner | Reported To |
|----------------|-------|------------|
| Operational health (current state) | SRE Lead / Service Owners | VP of Operations (weekly) |
| SLA compliance | SLM Manager | VP of Operations (weekly); SLM Review Board (quarterly) |
| Program health | SLM Manager | VP of Operations (monthly); SLM Review Board (quarterly) |
| Commercial / customer | Account Management + SLM Manager | VP of Operations + CRO (monthly) |

## Metric Calibration Notes

**Availability % precision**: Report to two decimal places (99.93%), not three (99.927%). Reporting false precision implies measurement accuracy the SLI infrastructure cannot support.

**Credit amounts**: Report in absolute dollars, not percentages of ARR, for monthly tracking. Use % of ARR only for executive reporting where relative scale is the relevant frame.

**SLO review completion**: A review "completed" means documented, actioned, and signed — not just "held the meeting." Track by the date the review record was finalized in the catalog, not the date of the meeting.
