## 3. Error Budget

### 3.1 Error Budget Calculation

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| SLO Target | [X.X]% |
| Error Budget (%) | [100 − X.X]% = [Y.YY]% |
| Error Budget (minutes, 30-day window) | [Y.YY]% × 43,200 min = [Z] minutes |
| Error Budget (minutes, 28-day window) | [Y.YY]% × 40,320 min = [Z] minutes |

### 3.2 Error Budget Policy Reference

This service follows the standard Error Budget Policy (see [ch04 — Error Budget Policy at Executive Level]).

Threshold actions:
- **50% consumed**: Engineering lead reviews burn sources; weekly VP briefing begins
- **75% consumed**: Non-critical deployments require Engineering Manager approval
- **90% consumed**: Deployment freeze; daily VP briefing
- **100% consumed**: Full deployment freeze; all capacity to reliability work; VP initiates customer communication if SLA-backed

Error budget override authority: VP of Operations + VP of Engineering (joint)
