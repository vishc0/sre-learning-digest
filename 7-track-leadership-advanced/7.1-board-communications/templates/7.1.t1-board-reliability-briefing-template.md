# Template — Quarterly Board Reliability Briefing

> **Usage notes:** This template produces a 3-slide board presentation (plus appendix) for a quarterly platform reliability update. Follow the board test before finalizing: every slide must be parseable in 30 seconds. Delete all guidance text before presenting. Maximum 15 minutes presenting; reserve 5 minutes for Q&A.
