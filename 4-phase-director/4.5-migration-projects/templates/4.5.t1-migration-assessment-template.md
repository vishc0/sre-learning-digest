## Migration Readiness Score

### Complexity Score

Score each factor 1-3. Lower score = lower complexity = easier to migrate.

| Factor | 1 (Low) | 2 (Medium) | 3 (High) | Score |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|-------|
| Dependency count | < 5 deps | 5-15 deps | > 15 deps | ___ |
| Data volume | < 10 GB | 10 GB - 1 TB | > 1 TB | ___ |
| SLA tier | Tier 3 | Tier 2 | Tier 1 | ___ |
| Team familiarity with target platform | High familiarity | Some experience | No experience | ___ |
| Code quality / test coverage | > 70% coverage, low debt | 30-70% coverage, med debt | < 30% coverage, high debt | ___ |

**Total Complexity Score (5-15)**: ___

- 5-7: Low complexity — candidate for Wave 1
- 8-11: Medium complexity — candidate for Wave 2
- 12-15: High complexity — candidate for Wave 3 or later

### Urgency Score

Score each factor 1-3. Higher score = higher urgency to migrate.

| Factor | 1 (Low) | 2 (Medium) | 3 (High) | Score |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|-------|
| Cost to stay on current platform | Minimal incremental cost | Meaningful ongoing cost | Platform cost is unsustainable | ___ |
| Security / compliance risk of not migrating | No known exposure | Some exposure, manageable | Active compliance gap | ___ |
| Vendor / platform EOL pressure | No EOL in view | EOL in 2-3 years | EOL within 12 months | ___ |
| Strategic alignment | Not on roadmap | Loosely aligned | Directly enables strategic capability | ___ |

**Total Urgency Score (4-12)**: ___

- 4-6: Low urgency — schedule based on capacity
- 7-9: Medium urgency — schedule within program timeline
- 10-12: High urgency — prioritize, do not defer

### Composite Positioning

| | Low Urgency (4-6) | Medium Urgency (7-9) | High Urgency (10-12) |
|-|-----------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Low Complexity (5-7) | Schedule late waves | Wave 2 | Wave 1 |
| Medium Complexity (8-11) | Wave 3 | Wave 2 | Wave 1-2 with support |
| High Complexity (12-15) | Retain or defer | Wave 3 with dedicated team | Immediate — dedicated program |
