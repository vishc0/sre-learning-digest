# Vendor Contract Register

**Owner**: Director of Operations  
**Review cadence**: Monthly (full review); Weekly (check for upcoming decision deadlines)  
**Purpose**: Single source of truth for all vendor relationships — costs, renewal dates, SLA status, and renewal decisions  
**Sensitivity**: Internal — contains contract values; do not share externally

---

## How to Use This Register

**Monthly review tasks** (should take 30 minutes max):
1. Pull SLA actuals from ServiceNow or your monitoring platform — update SLA Status column
2. Check whether any "Decision Deadline" falls within the next 60 days — flag those rows
3. Update "Last Review" date for any vendor you touched this month
4. Ensure "Renewal Decision" is filled in for any contract ending within 90 days

**Calendar setup** (do once, maintain ongoing):
- For each contract: set a calendar reminder at `[Contract End Date] minus [Notice Period] minus 30 days`
- This gives you 30 days of buffer before the point of no return
- Example: Contract ends Dec 31. 60-day notice required. Set reminder: Oct 1.

**Renewal Decision field rules**:
- Must not be blank for any contract ending within 90 days
- Options: Renew / Renegotiate / Exit / Under evaluation
- "Under evaluation" is acceptable only if the evaluation is actively in progress with a decision date set
- Leaving a contract to auto-renew without a documented decision is a governance failure

## Field Definitions

| Field | Definition | Who fills it in |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| Vendor | Legal name of the vendor entity | Director / SM |
| Service Description | What they provide — specific enough to be unambiguous | SM (internal owner) |
| Internal Owner (SM) | Senior Manager responsible for day-to-day relationship | Director assigns |
| Business Domain | Which part of your org this serves (Reliability, Platform, Security, etc.) | SM |
| Annual Value | Total annual contract value in dollars, including any add-ons or variable costs | Finance confirmation |
| Contract Start | Effective start date of current contract term | Contract document |
| Contract End | Expiration date of current contract term | Contract document |
| Notice Period | Days of written notice required to cancel or not renew — read the contract | Contract document |
| Decision Deadline | Contract End minus Notice Period. This is the last day to avoid auto-renew. | Calculate |
| Auto-Renew | Does the contract auto-renew if notice is not given? | Contract document |
| SLA Commitment | Key SLAs in one line — e.g., "99.9% availability; P1 response <1hr" | SM pulls from contract |
| SLA Status | Current compliance status | SM updates monthly |
| Last QBR Date | Date of most recent formal QBR | SM updates after each QBR |
| Last Review Date | Date this row was last reviewed by Director | Director updates |
| Renewal Decision | Current decision status | Director decides |
| Notes | Anything material — price increases, open disputes, relationship flags | SM or Director |

## SLA Status Definitions

| Symbol | Meaning | Action required |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| ✓ | Compliant — meeting all contracted SLAs for the past quarter | No action — note at next QBR |
| ⚠ | At risk — one or more SLAs approaching breach, or one minor breach in the quarter | Raise in next QBR; SM to monitor weekly |
| ✗ | In breach — one or more material SLA breaches this quarter | Director awareness required; escalate in QBR; document formally |

If ✗ status persists for two consecutive quarters with no resolution: escalate to vendor executive, flag in renewal decision as "Renegotiate" or "Exit."

---

## Renewal Decision Definitions

| Decision | Meaning | Required actions |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Renew | Extend at current terms | Confirm terms in writing with vendor 30 days before deadline |
| Renegotiate | Extend but with changed terms | Initiate negotiation 90 days before deadline; have SM + Director aligned on target terms |
| Exit | Not renewing — contract will end | Notify vendor per notice period; initiate migration / replacement plan; ensure no service gap |
| Under evaluation | Decision not made yet | Must have a decision owner and a date — "Under evaluation" without a deadline is not valid |
