# Partner / Vendor Quarterly Business Review Template

**Usage**: Filled out jointly — your sections completed before the meeting, partner sections completed during or after the meeting  
**Distribution**: Both parties receive a copy within 24 hours of the meeting, with a request for confirmation within 48 hours  
**Retention**: Keep all QBR documents; they are the evidentiary record if a contract dispute or termination occurs
