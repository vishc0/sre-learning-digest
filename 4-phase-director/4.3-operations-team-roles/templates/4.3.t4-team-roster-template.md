**On-call status definitions**:
- **Primary**: Cleared to serve as primary IC for incidents within this rotation's severity scope. Carries the pager on primary on-call weeks.
- **Secondary**: Cleared to serve as secondary IC (backup and escalation support). Carries the pager on secondary on-call weeks. May not yet be cleared for P1 primary.
- **Observer**: In training — shadows incidents but is not the decision-maker. Should not be the only person on a shift.
- **Not yet**: New to the team, not yet eligible for on-call. Expected on-call eligibility date should be documented in their 90-day plan.
