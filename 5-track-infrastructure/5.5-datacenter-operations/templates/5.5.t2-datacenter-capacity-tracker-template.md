**Column Definitions**:
- **kW Allocated**: Reserved capacity per planned/approved change tickets (what's committed on paper)
- **kW Actual**: Measured draw from PDU management system (what's actually running)
- **kW Headroom**: Allocated minus Actual (stranded capacity if large; watch if near zero)
- **RU Used**: Rack units occupied by equipment (including blanking panels do NOT count)
- **Cross-Connects**: Number of physical cross-connects terminating in this rack
